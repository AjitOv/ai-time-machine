Most "AI trading" tools are a single black box.

One model. One signal. No idea why it fired, no memory of what worked, no awareness when the regime shifts under it.

Time Machine is eleven minds, voting.

Context gatekeeps. Behavior reads order flow. DNA recalls similar setups. Simulation rolls 100 futures. Decision arbitrates. Risk caps exposure. Meta watches the whole system for drift — and the entire stack rewrites its own weights from every trade outcome.

The intelligence stack

11 engines. One decision.

Each engine has one job. None is allowed to act alone.

01

Data Engine

Multi-source ingestion with automatic failover: Dhan → FYERS → Groww → yfinance. Five timeframes (1m / 5m / 15m / 1h / 4h) and live RSI(14), EMA(11/21/50), ATR(14) computed in-stream.

Source redundancy · 60s polling · 5 timeframes
02

Context Engine

The gatekeeper. Detects market phase (RANGE / TREND / EXHAUSTION / CHAOTIC), classifies regime, reads HTF bias from EMA(50) slope, and maps Fibonacci premium/discount zones.

Auto-rejects CHAOTIC phase · ATR-ratio circuit breaker
03

Behavior Engine

Detects liquidity sweeps, false-breakout traps, momentum shifts and volatility expansion — then scores confluence with context for an alignment number up to ±0.9.

MD5 pattern signatures · Replay-grade accountability
04

DNA Engine

The memory. Stores winning setups as 7-dimensional feature vectors and matches the present moment against history with cosine similarity. Reliability = win-rate × log(N).

Auto-decay weak setups · Confidence threshold 0.6
05

Simulation Engine

100 Monte Carlo paths × 50 steps using Geometric Brownian Motion, drift-biased by DNA direction (±30%) and σ-scaled by regime (0.9× trending → 1.8× news-driven).

90% confidence interval · Target/SL hit probability
06

Scenario Engine

Wraps simulation output into three readable futures — Bullish · Bearish · Neutral — sorted by probability, each with price target, dominant probability and auto-generated risk narrative.

95th / 50th / 5th percentile targets
07

Decision Engine

The final arbiter. Five gates must pass: context permission, DNA > 0.6, sim probability > 60%, uncertainty < 0.4, score |> 0.15|. ANY failure → NO_TRADE.

Auto entry · 1.5× ATR stop · 2:1 RR minimum
08

Uncertainty Engine

Measures signal agreement across engines (-1 = all disagree, +1 = all agree), tracks simulation stability and flags conflicts. Kills false-confidence trades (high score + high uncertainty).

Conflict detection · Stability scoring
09

Risk Engine

Dynamic position sizing 0.5× → 1.5× base by confidence. Hard caps: 2% daily risk across all trades, 3 consecutive losses = trading halted for the day.

Confidence-scaled sizing · Daily kill-switch
10

Learning Engine

Reinforcement loop. Every trade outcome rewards engines that voted correctly and penalises those that didn't. Engine weights auto-rebalance between 0.05 and 0.6, summed to 1.0.

LR 0.01 · 20-trade rolling stats
11

Meta Engine

Watches the watcher. Detects performance decline, regime instability and overfitting (recent vs all-time win-rate gap × 3). Auto-raises thresholds and slashes learning rate when needed.

Self-healing · CRITICAL/HEALTHY status
